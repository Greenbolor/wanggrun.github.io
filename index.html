Short Bio

Guangrun Wang is currently a PhD candidate in Sun Yat-sen University of HCP Lab, advised by Prof. Liang Lin. He is fortunate to work as a visiting scholar at CUHK MMLab for over 21 months, advised by Prof. Ping Luo and Prof. Xiaogang Wang. His research interests include machine learning and computer vision. He served as a reviewer for TCSVT, TIP, PR, JVCIR, and Acta Automatica Sinica.

He has published more than 10 papers with ~230 citations

Currently his GPA is 95, ranking 1st in his class. He ranked 1st in the '2015 graduate innovation funding of the High performance computing collaborative innovation center', where only 7 graduate students are awarded in China. He won several competition medals such as Kaggle golden/silver/bronze medal. He ranked 1st in PASCAL VOC leaderboard. He won two National Scholarships.

He obtained double Bachelor Degrees, including Engineering and Business.
